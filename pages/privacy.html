Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Financial Health Score ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit financialhealthscore.in.

Financial Data: Zero Collection

The most important thing to know: we do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any financial information you enter into our calculators. All calculations run entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your income, expenses, savings, and loan figures never leave your device. We cannot see them. We do not want to see them.

What We Do Collect

Like most websites, we use analytics tools (Google Analytics) to understand general usage patterns. This may include anonymous data such as pages visited, time on site, approximate geographic location (country/city level), device type, and browser type. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Cookies

We use cookies for analytics purposes (Google Analytics) and to remember your preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use our calculators.

Google AdSense

This Site may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to show ads based on your interests. You can manage Google's ad settings at adssettings.google.com.

Third-Party Links

Our Site may link to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. Please review their privacy policies before sharing any personal information.

Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact us through our contact page.
